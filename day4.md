## What is Fabrication?
Fabrication is the process of manufacturing integrated circuits (ICs) on a silicon wafer using a series of physical and chemical steps.



## Steps Involved in Fabrication
1. Silicon wafer preparation
2. Oxidation
3. Photolithography
4. Etching
5. Diffusion / Ion Implantation
6. Metallization
7. Passivation
8. Testing and Packaging



## Layout Design Basics
Layout design is the physical representation of a circuit on silicon.  
It uses different layers like:
- Active layer
- Poly layer
- Metal layers
- Contact and via layers

Design Rule Check (DRC) and Layout Versus Schematic (LVS) are important verification steps.
